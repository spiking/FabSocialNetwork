    Welcome to Flash Network

    Flash Network is a new and modern social network where you quickly and easily can share photos and ideas with the rest of the community. With a simple user interface, you can instantly share important moments of your life with friends and family. It also makes it easy to explore and interact with other users through comments and likes among other things.
